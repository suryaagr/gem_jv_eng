Welcome to the Shivir

This study guide encapsulates the core teachings of a 10-day Jeevan Vidya "shivir," a profound workshop dedicated to the process of "Consciousness transformation." Its core mission is to guide the individual from a state of open queries, confusion, and conflict to one of comprehensive resolution, fulfillment, and harmony.

The workshop, facilitated by a long-term student of the philosophy (*darshan*) of A. Nagraj, aims to provide clarity on life's most fundamental questions.

A Journey of Self-Exploration

The guide is structured to mirror the workshop's progression. It is a journey from the self, to the cosmos, and back to the art of living.
